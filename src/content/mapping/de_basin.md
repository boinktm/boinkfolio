**de_basin** is a compact wingman map designed for fast, aggressive 2v2 rounds. The setting is a sun-blasted dry basin in the American Southwest — cracked earth, sandstone cliff faces, and a derelict water pumping station that houses the single bombsite.

## Design Goals

Wingman maps need to get to the action fast. Both spawns funnel into the basin floor within seconds, but each path offers a different angle of approach. The T-side can push through a narrow canyon for a close-range entry or take a longer route over exposed high ground for an off-angle peek. CTs choose between anchoring the site or playing aggressively for early information.

## Detailing

- Hand-sculpted displacement terrain for natural rock formations
- Warm golden-hour lighting with harsh shadows from canyon walls
- Ambient dust particles and distant heat haze for atmosphere
- Minimal clutter — every prop serves a gameplay or navigation purpose
