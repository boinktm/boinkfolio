**de_railyard** is a competitive 5v5 defuse map built around an abandoned freight terminal somewhere in Eastern Europe. Rusted train cars, crumbling loading docks, and a lattice of overhead gantries create a visually dense environment that still reads clearly in fast-paced play.

## Layout Philosophy

The map revolves around a central rail corridor that serves as mid control. Both bombsites branch off opposite ends — **Site A** sits inside a partially collapsed warehouse, while **Site B** occupies an open switching yard flanked by stacked shipping containers. Rotations are fast but exposed, rewarding teams that invest in mid presence early.

## Technical Highlights

- Custom PBR material set for weathered steel, cracked concrete, and overgrown ballast
- Source 2 light probes tuned for overcast daylight with strong directional shadows through warehouse skylights
- Competitive-grade visibility — no pixel peeks, clear silhouette contrast on all engagement surfaces
- Optimized vis clusters keeping frame times consistent across hardware tiers
